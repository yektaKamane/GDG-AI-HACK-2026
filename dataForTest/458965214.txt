Boil salted water in a large pot and cook your favorite pasta until al dente. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic until fragrant, then add chopped tomatoes, salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili flakes. Simmer for a few minutes, then toss in the drained pasta with a little pasta water to coat everything evenly. Finish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese, and serve hot.
