Agentic AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can make decisions and perform tasks on its own with little human guidance. Unlike traditional AI systems that only follow direct instructions, agentic AI can plan actions, solve problems, and adapt to changing situations. It is designed to act like an intelligent assistant that works toward specific goals efficiently and independently.

One of the main advantages of agentic AI is its ability to improve productivity in many fields. In healthcare, it can help doctors analyze patient data and suggest treatments. In business, it can automate customer service, manage schedules, and support decision-making. Agentic AI is also used in robotics, transportation, and education to complete tasks faster and more accurately than humans in some situations.

However, agentic AI also raises important ethical and safety concerns. Since these systems can make independent decisions, people worry about privacy, job replacement, and the possibility of AI making harmful choices. Experts believe that proper rules, human oversight, and responsible development are necessary to ensure agentic AI benefits society while reducing potential risks.
